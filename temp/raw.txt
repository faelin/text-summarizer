<b>Can I customize the Share Stats page with my own logo?</b><br />

<br />

If you want to customize the Public Stats page for your tracking links or the Share Stats page of your rotator URLs, you can upload a graphic or logo of your choice to replace the ClickMagick logo.<br />

<br />

Just go to&nbsp;the &ldquo;Public Stats&rdquo; tab of your <strong><a href="/user/profile.cgi?cat=publicstats" target="_blank">User Profile</a></strong>, which you can always find the <strong>Account</strong> menu at the top of any page.
